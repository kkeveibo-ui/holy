ABOU BEN ADAM

abou ben adam o'may ur generation increase woke up in the middle of the night , woken up by the moonlight blooming like a lily in water saw an angel writing in a book of Gold what writtest thee asked adam , the names of those who love the lord said the angel, am i on the list asked abou ,ney replied the angel, abou said i;ll pray for them , then write me as one of those who loves his fellowmen the angel vanished and returns the next day and showed abou the names on the list wriiten in the book , and behold thee abou's name leads the rest.
